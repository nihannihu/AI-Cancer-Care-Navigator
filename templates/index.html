About This Platform

Onco-Navigator AI is a unified platform designed to address the critical gap in cancer care access in rural India, where 70% of the population lacks access to oncology specialists.

Rural Healthcare Gap

• Only 30 oncologists serve rural India's 70% population
• Average travel distance to cancer center: 150+ km
• Delayed diagnosis leads to late-stage treatment
• Poor symptom monitoring affects treatment outcomes

Emergency Contact: If you're experiencing severe symptoms that require immediate attention, please contact our 24/7 Oncology Emergency Hotline at 1-800-CANCER or visit your nearest emergency room.
