Dataset Information

Overview of all datasets used in the Onco-Navigator AI platform for cancer detection and diagnosis.

CBIS-DDSM Dataset

The Curated Breast Imaging Subset of DDSM (CBIS-DDSM) is the primary dataset used for training our breast cancer detection model.

Key Statistics:

  • Images: Over 2,000 mammography images
  • Annotations: Lesion segmentation masks and bounding boxes
  • Labels: Benign vs. Malignant classifications
  • Usage: Primary model training for AI diagnostics

Source:

National Cancer Institute

Breast Calcification Dataset

Detailed dataset focusing on calcifications in mammograms, which are important indicators for breast cancer diagnosis.

Key Features:

  • Type: Calcification abnormalities
  • Characteristics: Shape, distribution, and subtlety ratings
  • Assessment: BI-RADS assessment categories
  • Pathology: Benign, malignant, and benign without callback

Source:

CBIS-DDSM calcification case descriptions

Breast Mass Dataset

Comprehensive dataset of mass abnormalities in mammograms with detailed morphological characteristics.

Key Features:

  • Type: Mass abnormalities
  • Shape: Round, oval, lobular, irregular, architectural distortion
  • Margins: Circumscribed, microlobulated, obscured, ill-defined, spiculated
  • Assessment: BI-RADS assessment categories
  • Pathology: Benign, malignant, and benign without callback

Source:

CBIS-DDSM mass case descriptions

MIMIC-III Clinical Dataset

De-identified critical care database containing clinical data for predictive modeling and outcome analysis.

Key Features:

  • Data Types: Patient demographics, vital signs, laboratory tests, medications
  • Coverage: 40,000+ patients and 60,000+ ICU stays
  • Usage: Predictive models for treatment outcomes and side effects

Source:

MIT Laboratory for Computational Physiology

Note: All datasets are used in accordance with their respective licenses and ethical guidelines. Patient privacy is protected through de-identification processes.
